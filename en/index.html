About me

Music and Programming student. Guitar teacher. Music teacher. On the continuous search for progressing and improving day by day. If I know it, I share it. If I don't know, I learn. Won't stop until I find the solution to every situation I get presented with.

When I was 12 years-old, I started studying guitar with the guitarist Nicolás Greco. Thanks to his influence, I continued my studies in the Music Conservatory of General San Martín, 'Alfredo Luis Schiuma'.

While studying there, I was able to start working as a Music teacher at Primary Schools, High Schools and Schools for children and young adults with disabilities in Buenos Aires and Capital Federal in Argentina, therefore I now have almost 10 years of work experience in this field. I also had the opportunity to take classes with or participate in Master Classes with great musicians like Ariel Elijovich, Omar Cyrulnik, Miguel de Olaso and Javier Bravo. I got my Music Teacher Degree with Guitar orientation under Laura Faintein's tutelage.

After a few years break, I'm getting back at studying with constancy again, little by little. If you are interested in listening what I've been up to, click here.

Nowadays, I'm starting to walk down the Programming path. Lighting anew my old interest on computers, I've been taking part on Coderhouse's FullStack Developer career. I'm learning about different things, and I will be uploading projects for every one of them. If you are interested on seeing what did I develop and which languages I have been looking into, click here.
